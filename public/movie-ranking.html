The Best Movies Acording to Majo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wild Robot

It's a great story because it explores the connection between nature and technology through Roz, a robot that learns to survive and take care of animals on a wild island.

Big Hero 6

It's a great movie because it combines action, comedy and emotion in a story about friendship, overcoming grief and the power of technology to do good.

Hacksaw Ridge

It is a great film because it shows the true story of Desmond Doss, a soldier who saved dozens of lives in the war without using weapons.
